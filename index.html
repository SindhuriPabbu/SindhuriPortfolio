With a strong foundation in programming (C, Java, Python) and hands-on experience in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Robotics, I thrive on building smart solutions while staying deeply conscious of their ethical and human impact.

What sets me apart is my interdisciplinary mindset. My interests in psychology and literature enrich my technical skills by fostering creativity, empathy, and introspection—qualities I believe are essential in designing technology that serves people, not just systems. Whether it’s solving a coding challenge, analyzing an algorithm, writing a poem, or reflecting on user experience, I bring curiosity, clarity, and a human touch to everything I do.
